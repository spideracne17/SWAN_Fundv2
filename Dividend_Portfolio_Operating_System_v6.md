# V1 — Dividend Quality Scoring Engine

## Eligible Universe

### Preferred

- Dividend Kings
- Dividend Aristocrats
- Investment-grade balance sheets
- Positive free cash flow
- Qualified dividends

### Excluded

- Most REITs
- Most BDCs
- Most MLPs
- Primarily non-qualified income vehicles

Goal:

100% qualified dividend income whenever practical.

---

## Scoring Factors

| Factor | Weight |
|----------|---------:|
| Chowder Rule | 25% |
| Yield vs Historical Yield | 25% |
| Dividend Growth Rate | 20% |
| P/E Valuation | 15% |
| 52 Week Position | 10% |
| Payout Ratio | 5% |

Maximum Score = 100

---

## Chowder Rule

Chowder Number =

Current Dividend Yield + 5-Year Dividend Growth Rate

### Minimum Targets

| Yield Range | Chowder Target |
|---------|---------:|
| Under 3% | 15+ |
| 3%–5% | 12+ |
| Above 5% | 8+ |

---

## Yield Valuation

Relative Yield =

Current Yield ÷ 5-Year Average Yield

### Score

| Relative Yield | Score |
|----------|---------:|
| >125% | 100 |
| 110%–125% | 80 |
| 100%–110% | 60 |
| <100% | 40 |

---

## Quality Ratings

| Score | Rating |
|---------:|----------|
| 90+ | Strong Buy |
| 80–89 | Buy |
| 70–79 | Watch |
| Below 70 | Pass |

# V2 — Income Smoothing Engine

## Objective

Create predictable monthly income while maintaining portfolio quality.

---

## Dividend Calendar Groups

### Group A

March / June / September / December

### Group B

January / April / July / October

### Group C

February / May / August / November

---

## Monthly Target Income

Target Monthly Income =

Annual Dividend Income ÷ 12

---

## Monthly Coverage Score

Coverage Score =

1 - ABS((Actual Income - Target Income) ÷ Target Income)

Converted to a 0–100 scale.

### Interpretation

| Score | Meaning |
|---------:|----------|
| 95–100 | Excellent |
| 90–94 | Good |
| 80–89 | Acceptable |
| Below 80 | Needs Improvement |

---

## Income Gap Analyzer

Identifies:

- Lowest income month
- Largest deficit
- Required additional annual income
- Required additional capital

# V6 — Retirement Income Readiness Engine

## Purpose

Measure how close dividend income is to funding lifestyle expenses.

---

# User Input: Annual Expenses

This is a REQUIRED user-editable field.

Example:

Annual Expenses = $60,000

or

Annual Expenses = $90,000

The system should never hardcode expenses.

Users may change this value at any time.

---

## Retirement Coverage Formula

Coverage % =

Annual Dividend Income ÷ Annual Expenses

---

## Example

Annual Dividend Income:

$18,000

Annual Expenses:

$60,000

Coverage:

30%

---
